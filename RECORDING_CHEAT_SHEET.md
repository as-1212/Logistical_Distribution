## ⚡ Quick Actions During Recording

**Show interactivity:**
- [ ] Change Product filter → watch dashboard update
- [ ] Change State filter → charts recalculate
- [ ] Hover on map bubbles → show tooltip details
- [ ] Point to auto-refresh checkbox

**If you get stuck:**
- Pause, take a breath
- Say *"Let me show you..."* while clicking
- The app is robust — clicking anything is fine

---

## 🛑 Technical Backup Plan

If Streamlit crashes during recording:
1. Restart: `streamlit run smart-logistics-frontend/app.py`
2. The app state resets to Dashboard — that's your starting point anyway
3. Resume from where you left off
